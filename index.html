About

Born out of Los Angeles, Crown Valley is a gritty fusion of rock and funk, drawing inspiration from the raw energy of classic rock legends and the infectious grooves of funk pioneers. Since they began, they've brought raw energy and infectious grooves to the stage. Harnessing the ability to switch from intense, riff-fueled jams to slower, melancholic melodies, they have captured the attention of their fans. With many electrifying performances under their belt, they've become known for their blistering solos and irresistible hooks. Crown Valley is here to ignite stages and hearts with their bare-bones, rock-funk sound.
